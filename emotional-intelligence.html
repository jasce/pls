Emotional Intelligence Workshops

Emotions Matter! Emotional Intelligence is the capacity to recognize, understand & regulate our emotions. This is a super important skill for life! This is how we learn to live & resolve conflicts gracefully!

A healthy emotional management dramatically improves the:

  • Happiness levels in our relationships
  • Fulfillment in career & intellectual pursuits
  • Every day positive mood-cycles
  • Overall mind & body well-being

That is why, Emotional Intelligence is an important tool at PLS
